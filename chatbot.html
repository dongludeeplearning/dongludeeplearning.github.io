Training ChatBot Project Demo

Introduction

Video Volunteers is a non-profit organization, empowers marginalised people to tell their stories and create change campaigns, so that their issues come out from under the rug and become important threads in India’s development narrative. This project is undertaken in providing for support for this non-profit organization with the aim of addressing the lack of medical knowledge in underdeveloped regions of India. This shortfall leads to an alarming number of preventable fatalities each year. The non-profit organization has furnished us with relevant document data and I have developed a training chatbot to enhance the understanding of medical knowledge and local resources by volunteers and the local population. The chatbot will serve as an educational tool to improve medical literacy and ultimately, save lives.

The Training ChatBot faces four significant challenges that must be addressed in order to achieve its objectives. These challenges include the creation of a database from a pdf source, the implementation of a chatbot with a user-friendly interface, the delivery of high-quality results to meet its goals, and the maintenance of user engagement and retention. Four modules are designed toensure the success of the Training-Bot and its ability to effectively serve its users, we design four modules, namely User Interface, DB Creator, Manager, and Adapter. In consideration of the future development of the platform, it is possible that game modules may be introduced based on feedback design.
