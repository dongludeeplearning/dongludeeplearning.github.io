Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper presents GAGAvatar, a novel framework for generalizable and animatable head avatar reconstruction from single images. By introducing the dual-lifting method for 3D Gaussian reconstruction and combining it with 3DMM-guided expression control, the method achieves single-forward-pass reconstruction and real-time reenactment capabilities. Extensive experiments demonstrate superior performance in reconstruction quality, expression accuracy, and rendering speed compared to existing approaches, while maintaining strong generalization to unseen identities. The framework establishes new benchmarks for one-shot head avatar creation and opens possibilities for real-time avatar applications in various domains.
