High-Level Insights: Why it Works?

The effectiveness stems from decoupling "motion capability" (memory bank) from "motion selection" (audio guidance). By having a rich bank of possible motions and learning a sophisticated way to query them based on audio style, the model avoids the averaging effect seen in simple regression models and produces distinctive, styled animations.
