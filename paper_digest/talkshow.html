High-Level Insights: Why it Works?

The key insight is to respect the different nature of correlation between speech and different body parts. By using a VQ-VAE for the body/hands, the model learns a discrete codebook of plausible poses, allowing it to sample diverse motions that are consistent with the speech rhythm without being overly constrained to a single deterministic output.
