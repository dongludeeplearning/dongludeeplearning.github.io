Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper presents MGGTalk, a novel framework for monocular and generalizable 3D Gaussian talking head animation. By exploring depth and symmetric facial characteristics, MGGTalk constructs complete 3D Gaussian facial attributes from single images trained on monocular datasets. The framework achieves superior performance in both video-driven and audio-driven talking head generation, surpassing state-of-the-art methods across various metrics while maintaining real-time inference speeds exceeding 40 FPS. MGGTalk enables practical applications by eliminating the need for multi-view datasets or personalized training, opening new possibilities for scalable talking head animation systems.
