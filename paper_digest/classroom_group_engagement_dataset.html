Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper introduces the OUC Classroom Group Engagement Dataset (OUC-CGE), the first benchmark dataset dedicated to group engagement analysis in authentic classroom settings. The dataset contains 7,705 annotated 10-second video clips from 17 undergraduate students, achieving 97.8% classification accuracy with state-of-the-art video recognition models. By transcending individualistic paradigms and incorporating the ICAP framework, OUC-CGE establishes group engagement as a computable pedagogical construct, offering diagnostic insights for teachers and catalyzing research in socially-embedded educational AI systems.
