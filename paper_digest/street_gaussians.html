Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper introduces Street Gaussians, a novel explicit scene representation for modeling dynamic urban street scenes. By decomposing scenes into composable point clouds with 3D Gaussians and implementing 4D spherical harmonics for dynamic appearances, the method achieves photorealistic rendering at 135 FPS within 30 minutes of training. The approach demonstrates superior performance on KITTI and Waymo datasets, enabling scene editing operations and semantic segmentation while significantly outperforming NeRF-based methods in rendering speed and quality.
