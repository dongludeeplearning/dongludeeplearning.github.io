High-Level Insights: Why it Works?

Imitator works by "fine-tuning" a generalist model to become a specialist. Just as a skilled impressionist actor learns the general mechanics of speech first and then studies a specific person's mannerisms to mimic them, Imitator uses its pre-trained prior to understand speech-to-motion basics and then optimizes its parameters to minimize the error against a specific person's reference video, effectively "learning" their unique style.

# Personalization / Adaptation Phase
def optimize_for_identity(reference_video, audio):
    # 1. Load Pre-trained Prior
    model = load_pretrained_transformer()
    
    # 2. Initialize Learnable Style Embedding
    style_embedding = Parameter(shape=(1, D_style))
    
    # 3. Optimization Loop
    for epoch in range(adaptation_steps):
        pred_motion = model(audio, style_embedding)
        loss = reconstruction_loss(pred_motion, reference_motion) + \
               bilabial_loss(pred_motion, audio)
        loss.backward()
        optimizer.step()
        
    return style_embedding
