High-Level Insights: Why it Works?

The core success lies in the synergy between self-supervised audio features (which provide rich, robust phonetic information even without labeled 3D data) and the Transformer's ability to model long-range context. The biased attention mechanisms further tailor the generic Transformer to the specific needs of audio-visual alignment, ensuring lips move precisely in sync with speech over long durations.

# FaceFormer Architecture
class FaceFormer(nn.Module):
    def forward(self, audio, past_motion):
        # 1. Audio Encoding (wav2vec 2.0)
        audio_feat = self.audio_encoder(audio)
        
        # 2. Motion Encoding (Past frames)
        motion_feat = self.motion_encoder(past_motion)
        
        # 3. Transformer Decoder with Biased Attention
        # Cross-Modal Attention (Audio-Motion Alignment)
        # Causal Self-Attention (Temporal Consistency)
        output = self.transformer_decoder(motion_feat, audio_feat)
        
        return self.final_projection(output)
