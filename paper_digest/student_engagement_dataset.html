Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper introduces the Student Engagement Dataset, collected from 19 college students solving math problems on MathSpring.org, providing 18,721 annotated video frames for engagement detection. The dataset includes temporal head pose data synchronized with learning activities and serves as a benchmark for developing computer vision models. Deep learning baselines achieve up to 94% accuracy, significantly outperforming traditional head pose estimation methods. The work integrates gaze tracking into MathSpring for real-time interventions and establishes a foundation for personalized online learning support.
