This paper introduces HUGS, a novel framework for creating animatable human avatars from monocular videos using 3D Gaussian Splatting. By representing humans and scenes as explicit Gaussians with joint LBS weight optimization for deformation control, HUGS achieves state-of-the-art rendering quality while being 100Ã— faster to train and 60 FPS faster to render than previous NeRF-based methods. The approach enables novel pose synthesis and scene composition with only 50-100 frames of monocular video input.
