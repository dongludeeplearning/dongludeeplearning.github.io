Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper introduces SplattingAvatar, a hybrid 3D representation combining mesh-embedded Gaussian Splatting for photorealistic human avatars. By disentangling motion and appearance through explicit mesh geometry and implicit Gaussian rendering, the method achieves real-time performance with over 300 FPS on GPUs and 30 FPS on mobile devices. The lifted optimization approach enables precise avatar reconstruction from monocular videos, supporting both head and full-body avatars with state-of-the-art rendering quality and animation compatibility.
