High-Level Insights: Why it Works? é«˜å±‚æ´å¯Ÿï¼šä¸ºä»€ä¹ˆæœ‰æ•ˆï¼Ÿ

It works because InterAct addresses the fundamental data bottleneck in HOI research by combining large-scale consolidation with quality enhancement. The key insight is that physical artifacts in MoCap data can be systematically corrected through optimization, and contact relationships provide a principled way to generate synthetic variations. By establishing comprehensive benchmarks across six tasks, InterAct creates a standardized evaluation framework that enables meaningful progress tracking in 3D HOI generation. The multi-task learning approach ensures that motion and contact modeling are jointly optimized, leading to more physically plausible and semantically coherent interactions.

Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This work introduces InterAct, a comprehensive benchmark that advances large-scale 3D human-object interaction generation by addressing critical dataset limitations. Through systematic data consolidation, artifact correction, and principled augmentation, InterAct provides 30.70 hours of high-quality HOI data with rich semantic annotations. The framework establishes six benchmarking tasks and demonstrates state-of-the-art performance through unified modeling approaches. Extensive experiments validate the dataset's utility as a foundational resource for advancing HOI generation research. The comprehensive nature of InterAct, combining dataset quality enhancement with standardized evaluation protocols, establishes a new paradigm for HOI research and paves the way for more realistic and controllable human-object interaction synthesis.
