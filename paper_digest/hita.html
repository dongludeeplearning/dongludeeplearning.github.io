Conclusionï¼ˆç»“è®ºï¼‰

This paper introduces Hita, a novel holistic tokenizer designed specifically for autoregressive image generation. By introducing learnable holistic queries enhanced with semantic features from foundation models and a causal token fusion mechanism, Hita captures global image properties while maintaining compatibility with causal attention in AR models. The framework achieves state-of-the-art performance on ImageNet with FID 2.59 and IS 281.9, while accelerating training convergence by 2.1x. Additionally, Hita enables zero-shot style transfer and image inpainting capabilities without task-specific training, demonstrating the power of holistic representations in improving both generation quality and semantic coherence.
